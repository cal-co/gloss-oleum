liquid specific gravity

according to
McCain, William D., Jr. is

ratio of the density of the liquid to the density of water both taken at the same temperature and pressure.

oil formation volume factor

according to
McCain, William D., Jr. is

volume of reservoir oil required to produce one barrel of oil in the stock tank.

solution gas-oil ratio (GOR)

according to
McCain, William D., Jr. is

quantity of gas dissolved in an oil at reservoir conditions.
